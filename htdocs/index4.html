ITZY - Ryujin

Real Name: Shin Ryujin

Group: ITZY

Birthday and birthplace: April 17, 2001, Gangnam-gu, South Korea

Position: Main Rapper, Dancer, Vocalist, Center

Facts!

Did you know:

  • > Ryujin was scouted at a GOT7 fanmeeting by an agent who was "so pretty that I ended up giving her my phone number."
  • > Throughout her career, she's been known to keep her hair short, earning her the title "Short-haired Goddess" from MIDZYs.
  • > Ryujin also has another nickname from fans: Ryusungi, which is a portmanteau of her name and "wonsungi" (which is "monkey" in Korean).
  • > She's competed in the survival shows Mix9ine and Stray Kids as a trainee.
  • > Apart from being an idol, Ryujin has a short acting history, appearing as a character in the film "The King" and as a feature on BTS's Love Yourself Highlight Reel, appearing in the latter alongside fellow member Yuna.
  • > In ITZY's dorm, she was roommates with Yeji until 2021, when Yeji became Chaeryeong's roommate As of now, Ryujin has her own room!
  • > Ryujin is a talented dancer, being able to pull off dances like her iconic Wannabe shoulder dance.
  • > Ryujin has two cats named Byullie and Dallie.
  • > Not much is known about him, but Ryujin has an older brother!
  • > She's tried many bright hair colors throughout her career (such as pink, blue, and white), leading her to call her hair condition "trash" in one interview!
